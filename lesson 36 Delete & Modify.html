Modify & Delete

Modify

Modify ko hum Array ma changing kar na ka liya use karta ha
Example:

var ary = ["Nauman",18,"Male","BS(cs)"]

hum ary ma as ki age chang kar ka 17 kar nai ha to hum as ka liya Modify ko use kara ga

as lakna ka tarika

ary[1] = 17;
document.write(ary)

Delete

as ko hum array ka index ya array ko delete karna ka liya use karta ha

as ko lakna ka tarika

var a = ["Nauman",18,"Male","BS(cs)"];
delete a[1];
document.write(a);
