comparison operators

comparison operators ko do velue ka darmayan comparison karna ka liya use karta ha
as ki velue ya to true hogi ya to flase

  • ==

    • ka right vali velue equal ho left vali la
    to as ki velue true hogi varna to flase

  • ===

    • as ka matlab ya ha ka right vali vrlur left vali ka equal ho lakin as ki data type bi ak ho
    to as ki velue true hogi ta to flase

  • !=

    • as ka matlab ya ka left vali velue right vali velue ka equal na ho
    to as ki velue true hogi

  • !==

    • as ka matlab ya ha ka na to velue equal ho aro na an ki data type ak ho
    to as ki velue true hogi

  • >

    • as ka matlab ha ka ap ka pas do velue ha to ap na kaha ha ka agar ka

    20>10

    yani ka 20 bara ho 10 sa to ans true hoga

  • <

    • as ka matlab ha ka ap ka pas do velue ha to ap na kaha ha ka agar ka

    20 < 10

    yani ka 20 chota ho 10 sa to ans true hoga

  • >=

    • as ka matlab ha ka ap ka pas do velue ha to ap na kaha ha ka agar ka

    20>10

    yani ka 20 bara ho 10 aro anki data type ak hoto sa ka ans true hoga

  • <=

    • as ka matlab ha ka ap ka pas do velue ha to ap na kaha ha ka agar ka

    20 < 10

    yani ka 20 chota ho 10 sa aro as ki data type ak jasi ho to ans true hoga
